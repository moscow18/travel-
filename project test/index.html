Explore the Magic of Alexandria.

Alexandria is one of the most beautiful coastal cities in Egypt, known as the Pearl of the Mediterranean. With its rich history and refreshing sea breeze, it offers visitors a unique experience. From the ancient Qaitbay Citadel to the modern Bibliotheca Alexandrina, the city blends history with culture. Walking along the Corniche, enjoying fresh seafood, and watching the sunset by the sea make Alexandria an unforgettable destination.
